About eSquare

eSquare e.V. is a non-profit, non-governmental organization based in Berlin, founded in 2018 and run by young professionals and volunteers, who are developing projects to engage citizens in the democratic process and empower civic participation online and offline. The organization implements projects locally in Germany and globally in several countries across the world and is open for partnerships with organizations working in the same field.

The name stems from the letter e which represents digital participation and square, which refers to the multiplying effect that eSquare aims to create in the society. Square can also refer to the physical square, where several roads meet, and different cultures would find as a common place to be.




Our Fields of Work






Non-formal education

We work on promoting non-formal education, we focus on younger generations and equipping them with necessary digital skills to increase their civic participation in the society and promote mutual understanding between various cultures.

Fact Checking

We focus on promoting critical thinking among people by supporting fact checking initiatives, carrying out projects that fact check statements of media and political entities, and finally by promoting awareness and media literacy among media consumers.

Tolerance Promotion through Youth Exchanges

We work on several projects that facilitate the exchange of youth between different cultures and backgrounds and promotes tolerance and mutual understanding.

Current Projects






Kids & IT

The project aims at equipping young migrant kids in Germany with essential digital skills that helps them integrate in the German society and provide them with the basic technological skills needed for future employment.

Fact Checking and AI Research

In this project we research trends in digital content fact checking, research the impact of fact checking initiatives on information consumers by collecting and analyzing data drawn from various countries around the world.

Stronger Bridges For Better Social Inclusion

A 7 days( including one day of travel) of mobility of youth workers: Training course organized by Youth Inclusion Association in Istanbul, Turkey that gathers 30 youth workers.
